PORK

/

TOFU

Clear Soup with

Tofu and Pork

This soup is very popular with Thai children, it is often served for lunch at school. It has a bit of everything, meat, vegetables and a simple taste. Gairng Jud Tao Hu Muu Sab is also a great accompaniment to a spicy Thai meals. It helps to cool the mouth a little. It is very easy to make using simple, readily available ingredients. You can also add some extra vegetables like carrots or mushrooms to my basic recipe below.

Ingredients

300 ml water 150g minced pork
1 block of egg tofu
4 cloves of garlic shrimps
2 coriander roots
5 Chinese cabbage leaves
1 tbsp celery leaves, chopped
1 tbps spring onion, chopped
1-2 tsp sugar
1 tsp soy sauce
1-2 tsp oyster sauce
1 tbsp pork stock
Crispy deep fried garlic
10 black pepper corns

Watch video

Preparation

Step 1
Smash the garlic, coriander roots and black pepper in a pestle and mortar.
Step 2
Add the minced pork and mix well. Then add oyster sauce and soy sauce, stir and set aside for 20 minutes.
Step 3
Heat the water in a sauce pan and add the pork stock.
Step 4
Take the minced pork and make into small balls and drop into the suace pan.
Step 5
Add the Chinese cabbage and sugar, then the tofu.
